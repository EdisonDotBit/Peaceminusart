About us

Welcome to ARTMINUSONE. My name is John Arthur Daisy, an artist and painter. I'm passionate about creating beautiful pieces that reflect the beauty of nature and the world around us. I've been painting for over 16 years and love the challenge of creating something new and unique each time. The mission of ARTMINUSONE is to make art accessible and affordable to everyone regardless of economic means, background, or artistic experience. Through this website, I hope to share my work, my process, and my passion with you all. Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy your stay.
