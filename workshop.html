Learn with ARTMINUSONE

Come join us at ARTMINUSONE's art studio! Our workshop is located in Pasig City. Our instructor, John Arthur Daisy, is a professional artist with over 16 years of experience in the art world.

The workshop is designed to give participants a chance to explore their creativity and learn proper painting techniques. We will provide all the materials needed, including brushes, paint, canvas, and more. We will also provide light refreshments.

The cost of the workshop is 499.00 PHP per person. We hope you will join us and have a great time painting and creating!
